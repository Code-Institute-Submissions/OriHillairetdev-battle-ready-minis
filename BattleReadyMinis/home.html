OUR PRICING – PLEASE READ

All units in Warhammer 40,000 vary enormously in size, shape and all have differing amounts of detail. Whether it be a squad of infantry, vehicle, monstrous creature or an HQ character,
it’s always best to send us an email listing the details of the unit/s so we can give you an accurate quotation.

If you wish for an accurate quote please send us an email and follow the format below:

Which model/s you require painting
 1. If the models are assembled or unassembled
 2. Any magnetisation required
 3. Wargear options
 4. How many require painting
 5. Any specific colours/details you require
 6. Basing requirements (urban, desert, grass or any other specific base requirements)
 7. Any additional blood, poison, weathering, damage or glowing effects
 8. The time frame you would like them completed by
 9. Where the model/s would need shipping to
 
Once we have this information, we will send you a quotation along with an estimated time that we can complete the work by.
If you’re happy to go ahead with the quotation we will send you our payment details.
Please note that we require a 50% pre-payment before the work is carried out.
